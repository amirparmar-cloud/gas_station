Feature Competency Radar

Tax & Accounting

Competitors provide raw data exports. OmniStation auto-categorizes expenses via OCR and generates accountant-ready summaries for US tax codes.

Video Synchronization Key Differentiator

If the POS logs a $0 transaction or a 'Void', the system automatically clips the exact 30-second camera footage and emails it to the owner.

Autonomous Shift Fulfillment

If a 24-hour shift gets canceled, the system auto-SMS texts available staff. First to reply 'YES' updates the schedule and payroll instantly.
